# Department for Registration of Persons (DRP)

![](../../static/img/logo-DRP.png)

The Registrations of Persons Act No 32 of 1968 was approved by Parliament on June 22, 1968.  Department for Registration of Persons is established to implement the provisions of the Registration of Persons Act No 32 of 1968. This Act is in operation from April 5, 1991. Legal background of the Department for Registration of Persons is provided through Regulations, Orders and Announcements.

Website: https://www.drp.gov.lk
